VIP RESERVATIONS AND BOTTLE SERVICE

Looking to upgrade your nightlife? The Necto offers VIP Bottle Service for any sized event. VIP Bottle Service includes a designated VIP section for the entire evening, free admission and line skip.

Although Necto Nightclub is an 18+ entertainment venue our VIP sections are reserved for our guests 21 and up only. Guests must provide a valid, non-expired form of ID before entry into Necto Nightclub, valid forms include: Passport, Military ID, Government issued driver’s license or identification card, no exceptions.

Bottle Service comes complete with garnishes and mixers (3 per bottle excluding bottled water and Red Bull)

Catering also available in the DJ Lounge. All catering orders must be confirmed at least 1 week in advance, prices vary.

Please note, gratuity is not included in bottle service prices; a 20% gratuity will be added to all VIP reservations for services provided. - (Free admission non-applicable for ticketed events. Packages must be purchased and confirmed in advance to receive line skip and free admission).

Fees are subject to change for special events. All rentals are subject to availability.
