Necto Nightclub FAQ

What is the dress code?

Necto is dedicated to a unique, fashion-forward style and a fun, hip environment. Therefore, neat and fitted attire is required. We will continually strive to accommodate those whose style and imagination suit our environment, but ask that you please refrain from wearing the following:

  • Athletic wear (some gameday exceptions)
  • Excessively baggy clothing (no tuck-ins, shirts cannot pass your front pockets)
  • Headgear (exceptions for ball caps and stylish hats)
  • Joggers or sweatpants
  • Tank tops

Management has sole discretion over the dress code and may change the requirements at any time without notice.

What age do you have to be to enter?

Necto Nightclub is an 18 years and older venue. Proper ID is required (see 'ID Requirments'). You must be 21+ to consume alcohol.

What kind of ID do you accept?

Valid ID is required to enter the club. Driver's License, Passport, Military ID, and State Issued IDs are acceptable. There are no exceptions. For question about IDs, please check our FAQ Page.

What if I lost or don’t have my ID?

If you aren’t currently in possession of any of the IDs listed above, we are only able to accept government-issued IDs that include your name, date of birth, and your picture. Unfortunately, there are no exceptions to this policy.

Do you accept digital copies of identification?

No.

I lost something at Necto, how do I find it?

Please call our office at (734) 994-5835, Monday through Friday from Noon to 5pm. If urgent, feel free to leave us a message. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

Where are the pictures that were taken at Necto located online?

Typically, the pictures will be uploaded to the Facebook page corresponding to the night you attended Necto within a week.

Where should I park?

Necto does not have its own parking lot. However, there is always plenty of street parking and we are located next to a parking structure on Maynard between E. Williams and E. Liberty. For directions and more parking information click here.
