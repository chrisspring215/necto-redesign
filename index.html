ABOUT THE CLUB

Established in 2001, the Necto Nightclub is Ann Arbor's premier destination for nightlife entertainment. Located in the heart of Ann Arbor's entertainment district, Necto Nightclub serves as the nightlife epicenter for University of Michigan's campus and the city at large.

The Necto offers multiple weekly and special events to maximize your nightlife experience including the internationally known Goth-Industrial night Factory Monday, the unique concert and event driven Mix Thursday, the largest and longest established gay night in Michigan Pride Friday, and one of the hottest club nights in Southeast Michigan, Frequency Saturday.

As an award-winning, full-service nightclub, the club is proud to offer clientele a full range of entertainment options and amenities including VIP, bottle service and party packages to ensure your night is memorable, and the ability to make your own night through our private event rental.

The Necto Nichgclub is honored to be voted 'Best Nightclub' by the Metro Times, Current Magazine, Channel 4’s Vote 4 The Best, Detroit A-List and AnnArbor.com and 'Best Venue with Diverse Music' by Real Detroit Weekly.
