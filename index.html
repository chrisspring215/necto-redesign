WEEKLY EVENTS

FACTORY Mondays

Factory is one of the longest running Goth-Industrial nights in the US. Always featuring local talents spinning Goth-Industrial, EBM, Alternative, Cyberpunk and Synth-pop in the Main Room. Madisi plays bass driven dance music in the Red Room. Hosted by MC Yoda.

Mix Thursdays

We’re mixing it up each & every Thursday, with a blend of concerts by renowned artists, special events, unique parties, and even functions that you can book. Heard about a DJ at Necto? It was probably on a Thursday - check our calendar to see what’s happening this week.

FACTORY PRIDE Fridays

The One-and-Only Gay Night. DJ Jace in the Main Room spins the hottest Pop, Top 40 & EDM. DJ Digi Mark plays Retro 80's to Top 40 Pop videos in the Red Room. Hosted by Chanel Hunter & Jadein Black at the Largest weekly LGBTQ Party in Michigan!

FREQUENCY Saturdays

DJ Hardy & MC Yoda host the hottest club night around. The dance floor will be packed from the best Top 40, House & Hip Hop Tracks. Don't let your weekend down. Let's get Turnt!
