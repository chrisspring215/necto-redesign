NECTO NIGHTCLUB POLICIES

Dress Code

Necto is dedicated to a unique, fashion-forward style and a fun, hip environment. Therefore, neat and fitted attire is required. We will continually strive to accommodate those whose style and imagination suit our environment, but ask that you please refrain from wearing the following:

  • Athletic wear (some gameday exceptions)
  • Excessively baggy clothing (no tuck-ins, shirts cannot pass your front pockets)
  • Headgear (exceptions for ball caps and stylish hats)
  • Joggers or sweatpants
  • Tank tops

Management has sole discretion over the dress code and may change the requirements at any time without notice.

Door Policy

Management reserves the right to deny admission for rude, argumentative, or offensive behavior. Our priority is the safety of all our patrons, and decisions made at the door reflect this prerogative. Additionally, management has the sole discretion in regards to enforcement of our dress code and door policy, and thus may change the requirements at any time without notice.

Age Requirements

Necto Nightclub is an 18 years and older venue. Proper ID is required (see 'ID Requirments'). You must be 21+ to consume alcohol.

ID Requirements

Valid ID is required to enter the club. Driver's License, Passport, Military ID, and State Issued IDs are acceptable. There are no exceptions. For question about IDs, please check our FAQ Page.

Credit Cards and Cash

We accept Visa, Mastercard, AMEX and cash at the bar. Cash only at the door. ATM located on premises.
